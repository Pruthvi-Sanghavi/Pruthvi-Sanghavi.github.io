DaVinci Studios

Vinci studios: This general playground of innovation is all about using a mixture of art, science and technology to bring about a revolution in the entertainment industry. Using robots such as quadrotors, micro land based robots to decorate the background of stage shows with light patterns. Or using any other technology such as augmented reality, virtual reality, brain computer interface, software development, animation, human computer interaction to design products and services which are capable of influencing the lives of the people around the world. Vinci studios name inspired from the town of arts ‘vinci’ and the birthplace of renaissance polymath leonardo da vinci. The studios would be concerned with any technological advancement in the fields beginning from music, dance, martial arts, entertainment, prosthetics, biomimicry, videos, drawings, makerspace, robotics, software, hardware, wearable technologies, sports, medicinal science, education, business, virtual reality, autonomy, self driving cars, trucks, toys….
